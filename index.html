How It Works

Six steps from requirement to delivery

A structured, transparent process built over 12+ years of managing China imports for buyers worldwide.

01 / 06

Requirement

Share your product specs, target price, quantity and timeline — we scope the sourcing brief with you.

02 / 06

Supplier Search

We shortlist matching factories from our vetted supplier network across China's manufacturing hubs.

03 / 06

Verification

Business licenses, factory audits and production-capacity checks confirm every supplier is legitimate.

04 / 06

Negotiation

We negotiate unit pricing, MOQs and payment terms on your behalf to protect your margins.

05 / 06

Quality Inspection

Pre-shipment inspection against your standards, with photo and video reports before goods ship.

06 / 06

Shipping

Customs clearance and door-to-door delivery, tracked end to end until goods reach you.

About GazitHUB Trade

A sourcing partner built on trust, not transactions

GazitHUB Trade was founded to close the trust gap between international buyers and China's manufacturing base. For over 12 years, our sourcing agents have worked directly with factories across Guangdong, Zhejiang and Fujian to secure fair pricing, verified quality and dependable delivery.

We work with importers, wholesalers, retail chains and growing brands who need a sourcing partner that behaves like an extension of their own team — transparent about costs, rigorous about quality, and accountable for every shipment.

Our team is based on the ground in China, which means faster supplier visits, real-time negotiation and inspection that happens in person, not over email.
